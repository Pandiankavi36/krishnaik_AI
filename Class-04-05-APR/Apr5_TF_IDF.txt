Why TF(Term Freuqency)?

Because More Occurrences = More importance in that document

why IDF(Inverse document frequency) 
Because Common words are not useful


IDF = N/DF  without log

Assume 
Total document (N) = 1000
Case 1: Rare word
APpears in 1 document
IDF = 1000/1 = 1000


case 2: Medium word
Appears in 10 documents
 IDF = 1000/10 = 100

case 3: Common word
Appears in 500 documents
 IDF = 1000/500 =2 

problem :
 values are too extreme 
1000 vs 100 vs 2

log basically compress the number
 log(2) = 0.69

Now values become 
6.9 vs 4.6vs 0.69
=============================================
rate word dominates everything
 Model becomes unstable with out log



why multiply TF * IDF ?

Because 
TF - Importance in document
IDF - Importance in corpus


why log in IDF
without log:
Rare words get extremely high values


Log: Smooths values
Keeps balance


Log reduces extreme differences while keeping the importance order intact
Log compresses large values so rare words don't overpower everything.

Final verdict:

TF - Word importance in the given document
IDF -  Checking word importance in entire data.
